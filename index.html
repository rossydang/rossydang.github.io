Hi! I'm Rossy. I'm a 2nd year undergraduate at Georgia Tech majoring in computer science! As a first generation college student in my family, I'm passionate about helping people grow professionally and learn about the resources they have availiable to them that they can use to find a better job and create a brighter future for themselves. For much of my life, I've worried a lot about the future, thinking about how I would do things like support my aging parents financially, and my focusing on my career has always been a way for me to be hopeful about the future. I know scary it can be to start a career mostly yourself, and I want to let people know that they aren't alone.
